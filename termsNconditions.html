About MyOrg360

MyOrg360 is a startup initiated by the alumni of NIT Rourkela. Recognised by startup-Odisha, startup-India and ranked among top 10 startups, MyOrg360 aims to provide 360 degree solutions to organizations. Our prime focus has always been the seamless use of products by people from all spheres of society including non-tech organizations and common man. As a part of giving back to the society, we engineers intend to minimise the financial constraints in using technologies and that's our primary concern. Our business model has been designed keeping in view the absolute ease in collaborating with us. As the significance of the word "digitization" is increasing day-by-day, we as a society stand by the notion and will try to do our best to implement it. Digitizing the organisations and making lives simpler using technology is what MyOrg360 strives for.
