About Us

MyOrg360 is a startup initiated by the alumni of NIT Rourkela. Our prime focus has always been the seamless use of products by people from all spheres of society including non-tech organizations and common man. Digitizing the organisations and making lives simpler using technology is what MyOrg360 strives for.
