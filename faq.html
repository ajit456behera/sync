    6. Where can I download the SYNC Application?
    A. SYNC is completely browser based and supports almost all browsers and all platforms. However, we will be releasing our application soon.
    7. How do I connect to SYNC on my PC or MAC?
    A. Steps for Hosts:
  • Open sync.myorg360.com in any suitable browser like Google Chrome/Mozilla Firefox
  • Sign-up/Sign-in.
  • Put your own Room ID and Passcode in the form.
  • Choose either of the two modes viz. Webinar mode or Video Conferencing mode.
  • Start the session.
  • Allow access to your microphone and go for echo test. Thumbs Up. You are in.
  • Copy the link and send it to your invitees/participants via any social media/messaging platform. You can do this from profile page too before starting the meeting.


    • Steps for Participants:
  • Open the link given by your host in any suitable browser like Google Chrome/Mozilla Firefox
  • Enter Your Name and Email Address(optional) and Join.
  • Allow access to your microphone and go for echo test. Thumbs Up. You are in.

    19. How do I take attendance of participants?
    A. You can view attendees by clicking on Presenters List button which is present in Side Navigation Panel.
    20. Do I have to have a webcam to join on Sync?
    A. Yes, for video conferencing you need to have a webcam, however for webinars, it’s alright if you don’t have access to that.
    21. Can I use Bluetooth Headset/Earphones/Headphones?
    A. Yes. SYNC supports all types of Bluetooth Headset, Earphones and Headphones.
    22. How can I invite customers/participants to a meeting?

    23. Can I record and screen share meeting?
